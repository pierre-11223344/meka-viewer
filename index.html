__"Someone was a jerk in Eureka/pulled instantly/didn't set a PT"__

This sucks. No one likes it when other people are jerks, and we get that 100%. Not getting to a NM isn't cool either. However, there is an understanding that prepping a NM means the person who did gets to set a PT (pull time), with some rare exceptions (notably onvi and the support fate, which aren't prepped by people but by the system itself).

This does not mean "sweet I can instapull everything I spawn and just tell people to suck it" because that probably means you are the problem. When you prep something, it's highly recommended that you set a PT a few (real world) minutes in advance. If you choose not to, that's fine, but don't be surprised when people ingame treat you like a jerk for acting like a jerk.

If the person who prepped it instapulled it, that sucks but it's not worth bringing into the chat for a 2 hour long argument that will just end in "this is how eureka works and it sucks.". You're mad, you're frustrated, that's *okay*. Take a deep breath, maybe blacklist (temporarily?) the person who did it, or ignore them so they don't get the satisfaction. Most of the NMs give 5 - 10 crystals so you're not missing much, and there will likely be more NMs spawning soon. Giving them a reaction is just giving them what they want.

__"But I want to do bunnies AND get NMs!"__

That's totally valid and you can do that. *However*, you can't wait for a bunny fate to end and then get mad the other people in the instance didn't hold the NM for you. You've chosen to try to do a few things at once and sometimes it'll work and sometimes it won't. That sucks, but it's not realistic to expect the entire instance to hold off on killing something because you're doing something else instead of what they are. If you're doing this, it's recommended you take breathtaker to speed up your mounts.

Above all, just remember: https://i.imgur.com/T4WdraD.png

## DEFINITIVE/OPTIMAL EUREKA SOLO LEVELING GUIDE
**This is the fastest way to level. There are other ways but they are more likely to lead to frustration and burnout. Following this guide you should be able to reach level 60 in about one lockout of each zone.**

**Important tips:**
- Start on a monday so u have challenge log for anemos and then pagos on tuesday after reset
- Grab a kirin's osode/vermilion cloak for your job of choice (the tradein pieces 2 cryptic seals for kirin 3 damascene for vermillion can be bought off marketboard and traded to Nostalgic Fellow on Pier 1)
- Always have a harmony pot active while in zone (can be bought off marketboard)
- Get eurekan potions for sustain (can be bought at base camp provisioner)
- [Use the bestiary](<https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1Z3sDux_-UTf9LQ0DYuKvemXKP4DLK4K34Rtn72mAmpY/htmlview#gid=1696169989>) - mutates/adapts are important in zones past anemos
- [Use the maps to locate mobs](<https://ffxiv-eureka.com/>)
- [Use logograms once you have them unlocked](<https://ffxiv-eureka.com/logograms>)
- You do **not** need Notorious Monsters (NMs/FATEs) to level and you get no crystals from NMs that are more than one level above you. The higher they are than you the worse the xp from them gets. Leveling strictly through nms is the slowest way to level.
